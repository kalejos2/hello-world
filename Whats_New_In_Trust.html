<p><strong><span>Trust Status Site</span><br></strong></p>

</div>


<p class="slds-m-bottom--large ">The Salesforce <a href="https://status.salsforce.com">Trust status site&nbsp;</a>provides a unified real-time status and forward-looking maintenance information for most of our products. Use the site to check status at a glance or access detailed incident and maintenance information. To receive email notifications of incidents and maintenance posted to the site, subscribe to <a href="https://trust.salesforce.com/en/trust/trust-notification-user-guide/">Trust Notifications.</a> You can configure the notifications based on the instance and type of event.</p>


<p class="slds-m-bottom--large ">Performance Degradation and Service Disruption incidents that appear on the site impact multiple customers and must be at least 5 minutes in length. The site provides regular updates until the incident is resolved.</p>


<p class="slds-m-bottom--large ">Refer to the Frequently Asked Questions for more information about the site. Join the conversation with fellow Salesforce users about this topic in our <a href="http://sfdc.co/infrasc">Salesforce Infrastructure&nbsp;</a> Trailblazer Community group. For other questions, open a case with our <a href="https://help.salesforce.com/home">Support&nbsp;</a> team.</p>
